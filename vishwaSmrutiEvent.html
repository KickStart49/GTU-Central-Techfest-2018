About Event

The mission of Alumni-meet 2018 of VGEC-AA is to create a lifelong and worldwide community of alumni through increased opportunities for meaningful engagement in order to increase awareness, pride, participation, volunteer involvement, and philanthropic commitment to VGEC.

What is Our Goal?

The mission of the VGEC Alumni Association is to provide opportunities for VGEc alumni to participate in and contribute to the continued growth and development of the College, to recognize VGEC alumni contributions to the University community and to society as a whole, and to promote interaction among VGEC alumni through programs that enrich their professional and personal lives.

To Serve, To Involve & To Inform

  • Strategic Goal 1: Inspiring Alumni Pride and Support for the VGEC
  • Strategic Goal 2: Engaging and Involving Our Community of Alumni and Students
  • Strategic Goal 3: Leaving Lasting Legacies for Our Alma Mater
  • Strategic Goal 4: Establishing Effective Operational Foundations for Our Association
